Building a Dynasty: The Chicago Blackhawks – by Antony Rossi

Posted on June 20, 2015 by crusadernews

As the exciting 2014-2015 NHL season came to an abrupt end for the Tampa Bay Lightning, one very special team has continued their dynasty with the homecoming of their third Stanley Cup in 6 years. As you may know, the Chicago Blackhawks are the 2014-2015 Stanley Cup Champions! They have surpassed three teams before becoming the Western Conference Champions and finally went on to take down the Bolts and win the most coveted prize in all of sports, the Stanley Cup.
